# Attributions

SagaTalu is a fork of [SUMI](https://github.com/psychoplath9450/SUMI) 
by psychoplath9450, which is itself a fork of 
[Papyrix](https://github.com/bigbag/papyrix) by Pavel Liashkov (@bigbag), 
which is based on [CrossPoint Reader](https://github.com/crosspoint-reader/crosspoint-reader) 
by Dave Allie.

The core reading engine, rendering pipeline, and typography system are 
the work of Pavel Liashkov and Dave Allie. SUMI added the plugin system,
games, Bluetooth, and productivity apps on top of that foundation.
